JLM Financial Partners Team

Larry Meyer | Managing Partner

Mr. Meyer has over 40 years of business experience, including self-employment and leadership positions in many diverse areas including education, franchising, real estate, and finance. Mr. Meyer began as a franchisee for a family owned business; Leadership Management International, a company that markets management and leadership training programs and teaches companies how to set strategic goals and develop long range strategic plans. They now operate in over 60 countries market their programs in over 20 languages. Mr. Meyer was also the principal and Leader of expansion for the U.S. based retail franchise company, T-Shirt Plus, thatPlus, which operated primarily in regional mall locations. During his tenure the chain grew to over 500 US locations. Founder/Principal of JLM Financial Investments – Private Investment Company founded in 1992 that buys companies and develops commercial and residential real estate during periods of economic expansion as well as buying distressed assets during downturns. JLM recently purchased over $200 million of assets over the last 4 years including commercial real estate, senior housing facilities, residential real estate, and corporate jet aircraft. Founder/Principal of JLM Healthcare – Owns and operates 10 senior living facilities with approximately 1,500 beds in the greater Chicago area. Mr. Meyer has served on numerous corporate boards and has a long history of philanthropic work. Mr. Meyer is currently the Chairman of the Strategic Planning Committee for the Boys & Girls Club of Greater Austin, Texas

Trey Owen | Managing Partner / Founder of PF-TO Management

Trey is an entrepreneur with 15 years of experience building and monetizing businesses that maintain the pursuit of operations excellence. Trey is the founder of PF-TO Management which operates 12 Planet Fitness locations currently open in Texas. Additionally, 6 or more locations are scheduled to open by the end of 2015 with 40+ more in the development pipeline throughout Texas, Oklahoma and Kansas. PF-TO Management’s established Planet Fitness locations (open 12+ months) outperform the average Planet Fitness location by 50% demonstrating the impact of a “top down” culture of high standards and expectations at the management and operational level. Prior to becoming a Planet Fitness franchisee, Mr. Owen founded, developed and eventually sold Texas Computer Training Institute, a business comprised of computer training centers focused on certification training throughout secondary markets across Texas. Mr. Owen holds a Bachelor of Science in Management from The United States Military Academy at West Point. Additional professional training includes previous licensing in real estate, insurance and finance. Trey serves as President for the Planet Fitness Independent Franchisee Association.
