Portfolio Team

JLM Financial Partners is a Private Investment Firm based in Austin, Texas.

Founded in 2015 by Larry Meyer, JLM Financial Partners' primary focus is on the acquisition and development of Planet Fitness franchises.

About Us Contact Us

JLM Financial Partners prides itself in its professional, straight forward, and fair approach to interacting with other Planet Fitness franchisees. We make a special effort to customize each transaction to fit the individual needs and goals for each franchisee. We have a 100% success rate on closing transactions that we have put under contract to date.

MISSION

We exist to deliver quality, clean and affordable fitness within a Judgment Free environment while simultaneously producing superior return performance within the Planet Fitness ecosystem. This is accomplished by fostering a culture based on trust, kindness and respect of members, Planet Fitness Corporate, financial partners, employees and vendors.

VISION

We strive to earn the reputation of being the most member-centric, effective, collaborative and transparent owner and operator of Planet Fitness locations across the globe. We will continue to evolve and improve through innovation, critical thinking, purposeful focus and hard work within our work environment. This vision will allow us to grow and expand to become the largest and most successful owner and operator within Planet Fitness.
